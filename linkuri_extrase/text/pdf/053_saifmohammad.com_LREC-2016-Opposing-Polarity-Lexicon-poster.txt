1. Our Contribution 
We created a sentiment lexicon of phrases and their 
constituent words: a sentiment composition lexicon (SCL). 
Each phrase includes at least one positive word and at 
least one negative word—we call such phrases opposing 
polarity phrases (OPP). 
Annotations: 
• done manually with real-valued score of sentiment 
association using Best‒Worst Scaling technique; 
• are shown to be reliable.  
 
 
SCLs, such as SCL-OPP and SCL-NMA [1], are useful in
